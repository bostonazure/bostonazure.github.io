About

Boston Azure and North Boston Azure user groups combined forces to bring Virtual Boston Azure to continue our great mission in the virtual world. We are planning to resume in-person events when possible.

Boston Azure is a community-run group with the goal of learning about cloud computing with the Microsoft Azure cloud computing platform. We have been meeting since October 2009, making us the oldest such Azure-focused community group in the world. The group was founded by Bill Wilder.
